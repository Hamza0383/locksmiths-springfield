Lock Rekeying Services

Moving into a new home is exciting, but it also raises security questions. Previous owners, tenants, contractors, and real estate agents may have had keys to your property. Lock rekeying provides an affordable way to ensure you're the only one with access to your home.

Rekeying changes the internal pins and tumblers of your existing locks, making old keys useless. This process costs significantly less than replacing all your locks while providing the same security benefit. Our technicians can rekey all the locks in your home to work with a single new key, providing both security and convenience.

The rekeying process is quick and efficient. In most cases, we can rekey multiple locks during a single service visit. You keep your existing door hardware, which maintains your home's appearance while updating security. This approach works well for homeowners who like their current locks but need fresh security.

We recommend rekeying in several situations beyond moving. If you've lost keys, experienced a break-in, or ended a relationship, rekeying restores your control over home access. It's also smart after contractors, house-sitters, or temporary residents have had keys. Our mobile service makes rekeying convenient - we come to you with everything needed.

Deadbolt Installation and Repair

Deadbolts provide essential security for exterior doors. These locks resist forced entry far better than standard doorknob locks. If your home lacks deadbolts, or existing ones are old or damaged, professional installation or repair significantly improves your security.

We install high-quality deadbolts on front doors, back doors, side entries, and garage access doors. Proper installation matters - even the best deadbolt provides little security if installed incorrectly. Our technicians ensure deadbolts are positioned correctly, strike plates are reinforced, and all components function smoothly.

Single-cylinder and double-cylinder deadbolts serve different purposes. Single-cylinder deadbolts use a key on the outside and a thumb turn inside, providing quick exit capability. Double-cylinder deadbolts require keys on both sides, offering extra security for doors with glass panels. We help you choose the appropriate type for each door.

Existing deadbolts sometimes need repair. Common issues include stiff operation, misaligned bolts, worn cylinders, and damaged strike plates. We diagnose these problems and provide appropriate repairs. When repair isn't cost-effective, we recommend and install quality replacement deadbolts that match your home's style and security needs.

Master Key Systems for Homes

Master key systems provide convenient access control for larger homes or multi-unit properties. These systems allow different keys to open specific locks while a master key opens all locks. Homeowners appreciate the flexibility and control master key systems provide.

For large homes, master key systems mean you don't need separate keys for every door. You might have one key for exterior doors and another for interior rooms, while the master key opens everything. This organization simplifies key management and improves security by limiting who has access to certain areas.

Multi-generational homes benefit from master key systems. Parents might have master access while children have keys only to their rooms and common areas. This setup provides privacy while maintaining parental oversight. Similarly, homeowners with in-law apartments can control access appropriately.

Our team designs master key systems tailored to your specific needs. We assess your property, discuss your access control goals, and create a system that works. Installation includes setting up all locks, cutting the necessary keys, and testing the entire system to ensure proper function.

Smart Lock Installation and Programming

Smart lock technology has transformed home security. These electronic locks offer keyless entry through smartphones, keypads, or biometric scanners. We install and program all major smart lock brands, helping homeowners benefit from modern security technology.

Smart locks eliminate concerns about lost keys or unauthorized key duplication. You control access through your smartphone, granting or revoking entry permissions remotely. This capability is perfect for letting in service providers, managing rental properties, or giving temporary access to guests.

Many smart locks integrate with home automation systems. You can program locks to engage automatically when you leave, receive notifications when doors are opened, and control access from anywhere in the world. Some models include video cameras, adding visual verification to your security system.

Installation requires proper setup and programming. Our technicians ensure smart locks are installed correctly, programmed to your specifications, and fully integrated with your smartphone or home automation system. We also teach you how to use all features, manage access codes, and troubleshoot common issues.

High-Security Lock Systems

Standard residential locks provide basic security but can be vulnerable to picking, bumping, and forced entry. High-security locks offer superior protection through advanced design and construction. These locks are ideal for homes in higher-risk areas or for homeowners who prioritize maximum security.

High-security locks feature specialized key designs that prevent unauthorized duplication. Many use patent-protected key blanks available only through authorized dealers. This control over key copying significantly reduces the risk of someone making copies without your knowledge.

Construction quality distinguishes high-security locks from standard models. Hardened steel components resist drilling and forcing. Advanced pin configurations defeat picking attempts. Some models include drill-resistant plates and reinforced bolt mechanisms. These features make unauthorized entry extremely difficult.

We carry and install high-security locks from trusted manufacturers. Our service includes consultation about which level of security best suits your needs and budget. While high-security locks cost more initially, they provide long-term value through superior protection and durability.

Lock Repair and Replacement

Locks eventually wear out or break, requiring repair or replacement. Ignoring lock problems compromises your home security and can lead to lockouts. Our technicians diagnose lock issues and provide cost-effective solutions that restore proper function and security.

Common lock problems include keys that stick or turn roughly, locks that don't latch properly, loose door handles, and cylinders that won't turn. Many of these issues can be repaired without replacing the entire lock. We assess each situation and recommend the most economical solution.

Sometimes replacement makes more sense than repair. Very old locks, severely damaged locks, or locks that have been compromised by break-in attempts should be replaced. We help you select replacement locks that match your home's style while meeting modern security standards.

Emergency lock repairs happen when locks fail completely, leaving doors that won't open or won't secure. Our mobile service responds quickly to these situations. We arrive with common replacement parts and can often complete repairs on the spot, restoring your home's security without delay.

Key Duplication and Cutting

Having spare house keys prevents lockouts and provides convenience for family members. Our service includes professional key cutting for all types of residential keys. We maintain quality key blanks and use precision cutting equipment to ensure copies work smoothly.

Standard house keys are straightforward to duplicate, but accuracy matters. Poorly cut copies can damage locks or fail to work properly. We cut keys precisely, test them in locks when possible, and guarantee they work correctly. This attention to detail prevents frustration and potential lock damage.

High-security and restricted keys require special authorization and equipment to duplicate. If you have these keys, bring proper identification and authorization. We verify your right to obtain copies before cutting restricted keys, maintaining the security these key systems provide.

We recommend keeping spare keys in secure locations - with trusted neighbors, at work, or in lockbox systems. However, avoid hiding keys outside under mats, rocks, or planters. These common hiding spots are well-known to burglars. Our technicians can suggest better solutions for emergency key access.

Window Lock Installation

Windows often receive less security attention than doors, but they're common entry points for burglaries. Proper window locks significantly improve home security. We install various window lock types suited to different window styles and security needs.

Standard window locks on many homes are minimal and easily defeated. Upgrading to higher-quality window locks deters break-ins and may qualify you for home insurance discounts. We install locks on sliding windows, double-hung windows, casement windows, and specialty window types.

Sliding windows benefit from track locks that prevent the window from sliding open. These locks are simple but effective, and we can install them quickly on multiple windows. Some homeowners also want keyed window locks for extra security, particularly on ground-floor windows.

Child safety is another consideration for window locks. We install locks that secure windows while allowing ventilation. These systems prevent windows from opening wide enough for children to fall through while permitting air circulation. Proper window security balances safety, ventilation, and security concerns.

Door Hardware Installation and Repair

Beyond locks, door hardware affects both function and security. We install and repair all types of residential door hardware including handles, knobs, hinges, closers, and strike plates. Proper hardware installation ensures doors operate smoothly while maintaining security.

Door handles and knobs wear out over time. Loose handles, sticky mechanisms, and broken springs all signal the need for repair or replacement. We install quality door hardware that matches your home's style while providing reliable performance. Our selection includes traditional designs and modern styles.

Hinges seem simple but play crucial security roles. Doors with outward-swinging configurations have exposed hinges that can be exploited. We install security hinges with non-removable pins or recommend reversing door swing direction when appropriate. These solutions prevent intruders from simply removing hinge pins to gain entry.

Strike plates are metal plates on door frames where lock bolts engage. Standard strike plates use short screws that don't provide much resistance to force. We install reinforced strike plates with long screws that anchor into wall studs. This simple upgrade dramatically increases door security against kick-in attempts.

Mailbox security prevents identity theft and mail fraud. If your mailbox lock is broken or you've lost keys, we can rekey or replace the lock. We can pick, rekey, or replace cabinet locks to restore access. Our service preserves the cabinet itself while solving the lock issue.

Gun safes, jewelry boxes, and other secure storage sometimes need lock service. While we don't handle all safe work, we can assist with many residential safe lock issues. If your situation requires specialized safe expertise, we'll refer you to appropriate specialists while handling your other needs.
