Transponder Key Programming

Modern vehicles use transponder technology for theft prevention. These keys contain electronic chips that must communicate correctly with your vehicle's security system. Simply cutting a new key blade isn't enough - the transponder must be programmed to your specific vehicle.

Our service includes complete transponder key programming for all makes and models. We use manufacturer-level diagnostic tools to access your vehicle's computer system and program new transponder chips. This process ensures your new key works seamlessly with your car's ignition and security features.

Transponder programming requires specialized knowledge and equipment. Many locksmiths lack the tools or training to handle modern automotive security systems. We invest in the latest programming technology and ongoing technician training. We can program keys for vehicles from the 1990s through current model years.

Failed programming attempts can sometimes cause issues with your vehicle's security system. That's why choosing an experienced professional matters. We follow proper procedures, use correct equipment, and guarantee our programming work. If we program a key, it will work correctly with your vehicle.

Key Fob Replacement and Repair

Key fobs provide convenient keyless entry and remote start capabilities. When these fobs fail, get lost, or break, you lose access to important vehicle features. We provide complete key fob replacement and repair services for all vehicle types.

Replacement fobs can be expensive through dealerships. Our service offers quality replacement fobs at competitive prices. We source OEM and aftermarket fobs, programming them to work perfectly with your vehicle. Most fob replacements can be completed during a single service call.

Sometimes fobs just need repair rather than replacement. Battery replacement is the most common fix, but we also repair broken buttons, damaged cases, and internal component failures. Our technicians diagnose fob issues and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

Programming new fobs requires accessing your vehicle's computer system. We handle this programming process efficiently, ensuring your new or repaired fob communicates properly with your car. Remote start, panic buttons, trunk release, and all other fob functions will work as designed.

Ignition Repair and Replacement

Ignition problems prevent your vehicle from starting, leaving you stranded. Common ignition issues include worn cylinders, broken keys stuck in the ignition, and failed ignition switches. Our technicians diagnose and repair all types of ignition problems.

Worn ignition cylinders are common in older vehicles or cars with high mileage. The constant turning of keys gradually wears down the internal pins and tumblers. We can repair or replace worn ignitions, restoring smooth key operation. This service also includes cutting new keys to match your repaired or replaced ignition.

Keys broken off in the ignition require careful extraction. Improper removal attempts can push the broken piece deeper or damage the ignition cylinder. Our technicians use specialized extraction tools to safely remove broken keys without causing additional damage. Once extracted, we can cut a new key and assess whether the ignition needs repair.

Ignition switch replacement becomes necessary when the electrical components fail. We handle complete switch replacement, ensuring proper installation and function. This repair restores your ability to start your vehicle and operate electrical systems controlled by the ignition switch.

Smart Key and Push-to-Start Systems

Smart key technology allows you to start your vehicle without inserting a key into the ignition. These proximity-based systems detect the smart key's presence and enable push-button starting. While convenient, smart key systems require specialized knowledge for replacement and programming.

We work with smart key systems from all major manufacturers. We can create new smart keys, program replacement keys, and diagnose system malfunctions. Our mobile service brings the necessary programming equipment to your location, providing dealership-quality service without the dealership price.

Lost smart keys present unique challenges since the vehicle may not start without the proximity signal. We can program new smart keys even when all existing keys are lost. This process involves accessing your vehicle's computer system and registering new keys to the security protocol.

Push-to-start systems sometimes experience electronic glitches or component failures. We diagnose these issues and provide appropriate repairs. Whether the problem involves the start button itself, the smart key system, or related electrical components, our team has the expertise to fix it.

Automotive Lock Repair

Car door locks, trunk locks, and other automotive locking mechanisms sometimes fail or become damaged. Broken locks compromise your vehicle's security and can leave you unable to access your car. We repair and replace all types of automotive locks.

Door lock actuators control the locking and unlocking of power door locks. When these actuators fail, doors won't lock or unlock properly. We replace faulty actuators and ensure your power locks work correctly. This repair restores both convenience and security.

Frozen locks are common during winter months in Massachusetts. Moisture enters the lock cylinder and freezes, preventing key insertion or turning. We safely thaw frozen locks without damaging the mechanism. We also provide preventive treatments to reduce future freezing issues.

Vandalism or break-in attempts can damage door locks and ignition cylinders. We repair this damage, restoring security to your vehicle. In cases where locks are severely damaged, we provide replacement locks and cut new keys to match. Our goal is to return your vehicle to full security and functionality.

Broken Key Extraction

Keys break in locks due to wear, forcing, or manufacturing defects. When a key breaks off in your car door, trunk, or ignition, attempting removal yourself often makes the problem worse. Our technicians safely extract broken keys without damaging your vehicle's locks.

Professional extraction tools allow us to grip and remove broken key pieces carefully. We assess the lock's condition after extraction and can often reuse the existing cylinder. If the lock suffered damage, we provide repair or replacement as needed.

Once we've extracted the broken key piece, we can use it to cut a new key. If the broken piece is too damaged, we can create a new key using your vehicle's key code or by decoding the existing lock cylinder. You'll drive away with a working key and undamaged locks.

Prevention is worth considering. If your key shows signs of wear or bending, have it replaced before it breaks. We can inspect your keys during any service call and advise you if replacement is recommended. A new key costs far less than dealing with a broken key emergency.
