Master Key Systems for Businesses

Master key systems provide essential access control for business properties. These systems allow different keys to open specific areas while master keys grant access to all locks. Business owners and managers need access to all areas, but employees should only enter authorized zones. One master key opens everything, while sub-master keys access specific departments, and individual keys work only for assigned areas.

Installing a master key system eliminates carrying dozens of keys. Managers move throughout facilities with a single key while maintaining strict control over employee access. This organization improves efficiency and enhances security by limiting unauthorized entry to sensitive areas. Our service designs custom master key systems tailored to your business structure, including configuring all locks, cutting necessary keys, and providing complete system documentation.

High-Security Lock Systems

Business properties require locks that withstand heavy use and resist unauthorized entry. High-security locks feature reinforced construction, pick-resistant cylinders, and key control systems that prevent unauthorized duplication. These advanced locks use patented key designs available only through authorized dealers, eliminating the risk of employees making unauthorized key copies.

Different business types need different security solutions. Retail stores benefit from locks allowing quick emergency opening while maintaining security. Offices need systems balancing convenience with protection. We carry locks from trusted manufacturers and help you select appropriate security levels for each access point in your facility.

Access Control Systems

Modern access control systems provide superior security and management capabilities. Electronic systems use key cards, fobs, or smartphone credentials to control entry, tracking who enters and exits, when they arrive, and which doors they access. Access control eliminates concerns about lost keys or unauthorized duplication. When an employee leaves, you simply deactivate their credentials.

Advanced access control integrates with cameras, alarms, and time-tracking software. Program doors to unlock automatically at opening time and secure at closing. Receive instant alerts when unauthorized access attempts occur. Our team installs and programs complete access control systems, handling everything from hardware installation to software configuration and staff training.

Panic Bar Installation and Service

Business buildings require panic bars on emergency exits to comply with fire codes and safety regulations. These devices allow quick exit during emergencies while preventing unauthorized entry from outside. Proper installation and maintenance protects lives and ensures code compliance.

Different situations require specific panic bar types. Rim exit devices mount on door surfaces, while mortise exit devices integrate into door edges. Some applications need panic bars with alarm features. Our service follows manufacturer specifications and local codes to ensure reliable, compliant installations that protect your business and occupants.

Lock Rekeying Services

Employee turnover, lost keys, and security breaches all require lock rekeying. This process changes lock cylinders so old keys no longer work, costing significantly less than replacing all locks while providing the same security benefit. Many businesses rekey regularly after employees leave, especially those with access to sensitive areas.

Our mobile service brings everything needed to rekey locks on-site, minimizing business disruption. We can rekey entire facilities during off-hours. Large facilities benefit from systematic rekeying that maintains master key system integrity, with complete documentation and key record updates.

Safe Sales and Service

Businesses need secure storage for cash, documents, and valuable items. Safes provide this protection. Fire-rated safes protect documents from fire damage, while burglary-rated safes resist forced entry. Our service includes safe consultation, delivery, installation, and ongoing maintenance.

Lost combinations, jammed mechanisms, and forgotten codes prevent safe access. Our technicians safely open safes without destroying them, then repair or replace components and reset combinations. Regular safe maintenance prevents problems and ensures reliable operation when needed.

Door Repair and Installation

Business doors endure heavy use and need periodic repair or replacement. Damaged doors compromise security. Our service includes door repair and installation for metal doors, glass doors, and specialty entries. We diagnose door problems like broken closers, damaged frames, and worn weatherstripping, providing cost-effective repairs.

Accessibility requirements mandate specific door hardware and automatic operators for buildings. We install ADA-compliant door hardware, automatic door operators, and push-button systems that meet regulatory standards while maintaining security.

Keyless Entry Systems

Keyless entry eliminates traditional keys in favor of keypads, card readers, or biometric scanners. These systems provide convenience and enhanced security, allowing employees to access facilities using codes or credentials that can be easily managed and changed. Keyless systems track entry activity, providing detailed logs of who entered and when.

Converting to keyless entry requires proper installation. Our team handles complete system implementation including hardware installation, software setup, credential programming, and user training. System flexibility allows different access levels for different users, with managers having 24/7 access while other employees can only enter during business hours.
